Privacy Policy for MajuRun

Effective Date: February 14, 2026

MajuRun ("we", "us", or "our") operates the MajuRun mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our App. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

We collect, use, or share **very limited information** — primarily only what is necessary for the App to function on your device. We do not sell your data, use it for advertising, or share it with third parties except as described below.

1. Information We Access or Collect

  • Device Permissions (e.g., CAMERA): The App may request access to your device's camera. This permission is used solely for on-device features (if applicable in MajuRun). We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any photos, videos, or camera data off your device unless you explicitly choose to save or share content yourself using your device's built-in tools.
  • In-App Purchases & Subscriptions: All payments and subscriptions are processed securely through Google Play Billing. We do not collect, store, or have access to your payment card details, billing address, or other financial information. Google handles all transaction processing and provides us only with limited purchase confirmation data (e.g., order ID, subscription status) needed to unlock features in the App.
  • No Other Personal Data: We do not collect personal information such as your name, email address, location, contacts, device identifiers, usage analytics, or any other data unless you voluntarily provide it (e.g., by contacting support). The App does not include tracking, advertising SDKs, or third-party analytics that collect user data.

2. How We Use Information

Any limited information we access is used only to provide and improve the core functionality of MajuRun, such as enabling in-app features or managing your subscription status. We do not use data for marketing, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to the App's core features.

3. Information Sharing

We do not share your personal information with third parties except:

  • With Google (for Play Billing subscription processing and App distribution) — subject to Google's privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy.
  • As required by law, to protect our rights, or in response to valid legal requests.

We do not sell your data to anyone.

4. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect any limited data we process. All communication with Google Play services uses secure, encrypted channels (HTTPS/TLS). However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

5. Data Retention

We retain only the minimal data necessary for App functionality (e.g., subscription status via Google). If no data is collected, nothing is retained by us.

6. Your Data Deletion Rights

If you want to delete your MajuRun account and any associated data:

  1. Email us at admin@majurun.com with your request. Please include your linked email address or user ID (if applicable).
  2. We will process your deletion request within 30 days and send you a confirmation.

Alternatively, if your account is linked to Facebook (if applicable), you can revoke access via Facebook Settings → Apps and Websites → MajuRun → Remove.

You can also uninstall the App and revoke any granted permissions through your device settings at any time.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the revised version here with a new effective date. For significant changes, we may notify you in the App or via other means. Please review this page periodically.
