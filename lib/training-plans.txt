Tips for success:

Run at an easy, conversational pace (you should be able to talk).
Warm up with 5 min brisk walk + dynamic stretches; cool down the same.
If a week feels too hard, repeat it — no rush.
Hydrate, eat well, and maybe add simple strength (squats, planks) 1-2×/week for injury prevention.
Track your runs (app like Strava/Nike Run Club) to see progress.
