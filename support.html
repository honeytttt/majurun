Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start tracking a run?

Open MajuRun, tap the "Start Run" button on the home screen. The app will begin tracking your location, distance, pace, and time automatically.

Why isn't my location tracking accurately?

Ensure location permissions are set to "Always" in your device settings. For best results, run in open areas away from tall buildings. GPS accuracy improves after the first minute.

How do I sync with Apple Health?

Go to Profile → Settings → Health Integration. Toggle on the data types you want to sync. You can also manage permissions in iOS Settings → Health → Data Access → MajuRun.

Can I use MajuRun offline?

Yes! MajuRun tracks your runs using GPS even without internet. Your data will sync to the cloud once you're back online.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Profile → Settings → Delete Account. Alternatively, email us at admin@majurun.com and we'll process your request within 30 days.

Is my data secure?

Yes. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We never sell your personal information. Read our Privacy Policy for details.

App Permissions Explained

  • Location (Always): Required to track your running route, even when screen is off
  • Health: To sync workouts and fitness data with Apple Health
  • Notifications: For workout reminders and achievement alerts
  • Motion & Fitness: For accurate step counting and cadence

Troubleshooting

  • App crashing: Try updating to the latest version or reinstalling
  • Battery drain: This is normal during GPS tracking. Use Low Power Mode between runs
  • Missing workouts: Check your internet connection and try pulling down to refresh
