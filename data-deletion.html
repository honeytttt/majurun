User Data Deletion Instructions

How to delete your data from Majurun

To delete your data from Majurun, please follow these steps:

  1. Send an email to hani.redhat@gmail.com with the subject "Data Deletion Request"
  2. Include your Facebook User ID in the email (you can find this in your Facebook profile URL or by contacting us)
  3. We will process your request within 30 days and confirm once your data has been deleted

What data gets deleted?

When you request data deletion, we will remove:

  • Your profile information
  • Your activity history
  • Any personally identifiable information
