MajuRun Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 22, 2026 | Last Updated: March 22, 2026

MajuRun is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

Summary: MajuRun collects health, location, and activity data to track your runs and fitness progress. Your data is stored securely and is never sold to third parties.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Health and Fitness Data (Apple HealthKit)

With your permission, we access:

  • Workout data: Running distance, duration, pace, calories burned
  • Activity metrics: Steps, active energy, heart rate
  • Historical fitness data: Past workouts for progress tracking
Important: Health data from HealthKit is used solely for displaying your fitness metrics and is never shared with third parties for advertising.

1.2 Location Data

  • Real-time GPS: To map your running route and calculate distance/pace
  • Background location: To continue tracking during a run
  • Route history: Stored to view past runs

Location tracking is only active during workouts.

1.3 Account Information

  • Authentication: Email address, name (Google Sign-In or email)
  • Profile: Photo (optional), display name

1.4 Device and Usage Data

  • Device info: Model, OS version
  • Analytics: Features used, session duration (Firebase Analytics)
  • Crash reports: Error data (Firebase Crashlytics)
  • Motion sensors: Step counting, cadence

1.5 Media

  • Photos/Camera: Only when you add a profile photo or share a workout
  • Microphone: Only if you record video with audio

1.6 Payment Information

Payments are handled by Apple/Google. We only receive subscription status, not payment details.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide core features: Track runs, display routes, calculate statistics
  • Sync data across devices
  • Improve the app and fix bugs
  • Send notifications (with permission)

We do NOT sell your data or use it for targeted advertising.

4. How We Share Your Information

  • Service providers: Firebase for analytics/storage
  • Your choice: When you share a workout
  • Legal: If required by law

5. Data Storage and Security

  • Data stored on secure Firebase/Google Cloud servers
  • HTTPS/TLS encryption for all transmissions
  • Access controls for user data

6. Data Retention

  • Account data retained while active
  • Workout history until you delete it
  • After deletion: removed within 30 days

7. Your Rights

  • View and export your data
  • Delete workouts or your account
  • Revoke permissions in device settings
  • Disable analytics in app settings

To delete your account: Profile > Settings > Delete Account, or email admin@majurun.com

8. Children's Privacy

MajuRun is not for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect their information.

9. Third-Party Services

  • Firebase (Google): Analytics, storage, auth
  • Apple HealthKit: Health data
  • Google Maps: Route display
  • Sentry: Error monitoring

10. California (CCPA) / European (GDPR) Rights

You have rights to access, delete, and port your data. We do not sell personal information.

11. Changes

We may update this policy and will notify you of significant changes.
