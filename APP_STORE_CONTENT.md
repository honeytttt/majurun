## App Icon Requirements

### iOS
- 1024 x 1024 px (App Store)
- No transparency, no rounded corners (system applies)

### Android
- 512 x 512 px (Play Store)
- Adaptive icon: Foreground + Background layers
- Foreground: 108 x 108 dp safe zone (432 x 432 px @4x)

### Icon Design Recommendations
- Primary: Neon green (#00E676) running figure silhouette
- Background: Dark (#0A0A0F) or gradient
- Style: Minimal, modern, recognizable at small sizes
