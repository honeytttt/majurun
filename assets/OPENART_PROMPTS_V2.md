# MajuRun — OpenArt.ai Prompts V2
**Revised**: April 2026 | **Credits budget**: ~12,000

> A complete redesign from V1. New aesthetic direction: **Dark Athletic Realism** — real human bodies,
> dramatic lighting, cinematic depth, neon green (#39FF14) as the only accent color.
> No cartoon mascots. No flat icons. Everything feels like a premium sports brand shoot.

---

## GLOBAL STYLE GUIDE

| Property | Value |
|---|---|
| Primary accent | Neon green `#39FF14` |
| Secondary accent | Electric blue `#00CFFF` (use sparingly) |
| Background | Near-black `#060906` or `#040404` |
| Human subjects | Diverse — South/Southeast Asian, Black, mixed ethnicity, male + female |
| Body type | Athletic but realistic — not bodybuilder, not stock photo thin |
| Lighting | Dramatic side/rim light, hard shadows, volumetric fog optional |
| Model recommendation | **Juggernaut XL** for stills, **Flux.1 Pro** for clean renders |
| Negative prompt (all stills) | `cartoon, anime, illustration, flat design, 3D render, plastic, watermark, text overlay, blurry, overexposed, stock photo smile` |

## 1. WORKOUT EXERCISE GIFs / MP4 LOOPS
**Platform**: openart.ai → Image → AnimateDiff or Kling AI video  
**Settings**: 2–4 second seamless loop, 512×512 or 768×768 square, no camera movement

These replace the CDN-hosted exercise videos. Generate each as a **looping animation** showing 1 full rep of the movement. The subject should fill most of the frame. Background is always dark (gym, studio, or outdoor night).
