Account Deletion & Data Deletion Authority

User‑Facing Summary & Internal SOP

A. User‑Facing Deletion Process

  1. In‑app: Settings → Account → Delete Account → Confirm.
  2. By email: Send a request from your registered email to admin@majurun.com with subject “Delete My Account”. We will verify your identity.
  3. Immediate deactivation: Account access is revoked immediately upon confirmation.
  4. Deletion timelines:
    • Profile, workouts, social graph, UGC: deleted or anonymised within 30 days.
    • Backups/DR: purged within 90 days on rolling cycles.
    • Legal holds: certain records (e.g., purchase history, fraud logs) retained only as required by law or for dispute resolution, then deleted/anonymised.
  5. Third‑party caches: Content others saved or re‑uploaded may persist outside our control.

B. Internal Authority & SOP

B1. Roles & Approval

  • Data Protection Officer (DPO): Owns policy, approves exceptions, oversees audits (admin@majurun.com).
  • Trust & Safety Lead: Executes UGC removals, blocks, escalations for harassment/illegal content.
  • Engineering (Backend/Ops): Executes data erasure in primary stores and backups; maintains audit logs.

B2. Intake & Verification

  • In‑app deletions use re‑authentication (password/biometric).
  • Email requests must come from the registered email; otherwise request further verification.

B3. Execution Steps (SDK‑Aware)

  1. Mark account DELETION_PENDING, revoke sessions/tokens, disable notifications.
  2. Delete Firebase Auth user; remove OAuth links (Google/Facebook/Twitter).
  3. Delete Firestore user document, posts, images/videos, and route data in Firebase Storage; purge CDN caches.
  4. Reset Firebase Analytics App Instance ID; invalidate FCM token; request Crashlytics deletion or rotate identifiers.
  5. Remove from indices/search caches; reindex to purge references.
  6. Log request ID, verifier, timestamps, datasets affected, completion hash.

B4. Timelines & SLAs

  • Acknowledge email requests within 5 business days.
  • Complete primary deletion within 30 days; backups within 90 days.
  • Confirm completion to the user with a summary of deleted/retained items and reasons.

B5. Exceptions & Retention

We may retain limited data where required or permitted by law (e.g., financial records, security logs), or to resolve disputes, enforce Terms, or prevent abuse. Retention is minimised and time‑bound, after which data is deleted or anonymised.

B6. UGC & Safety

For content involving harassment, doxxing, exploitation, or credible threats, Trust & Safety may remove content immediately and preserve evidence required for lawful requests.

B7. Appeals & Complaints

Appeal decisions or lodge privacy complaints with the DPO at admin@majurun.com. Unresolved matters may be raised with the Personal Data Protection Commission (Singapore).
